Welcome to the SleepBack Research Study

Mobile App-delivered sleep therapy (SleepFix) for people with chronic low back pain and insomnia.

You are invited to take part in an online research study using a smartphone application designed to improve sleep and daytime functioning.

SleepFix

SleepFix is a smartphone application designed to treat insomnia using Sleep Retraining Therapy (SRT)

SRT works to improve the balance between actual sleep time and the total amount of time spent in bed including the time spent both awake and asleep.

This study is for adults who:

  • Have chronic low back pain (pain in the lower back region for 3 months or more)
  • Have sleep problems
  • Have access to a smartphone

Pre-screening Questionnaire

To determine if you are eligible to participate, you will be asked to complete a short series of screening questions (~5 minutes). Upon completion you will know if you are eligible for the study.
