Welcome to the SleepBack Research Study

You are invited to take part in a fully online research study using a smartphone application designed to improve sleep and daytime functioning in people with chronic low back pain.

To understand what is involved in this study, please watch the video below.

INTRODUCTORY VIDEO

This study is for adults who:

  • Have chronic low back pain (pain in the lower back region for 3 months or more)
  • Have sleep problems
  • Have access to a smartphone
