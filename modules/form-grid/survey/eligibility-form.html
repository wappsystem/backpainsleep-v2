Pre-screening Survey

 The following survey will ask questions to assess whether you are eligible to partake in this study. If you are eligible you will be asked to submit your personal contact details so that you can be contacted in order to arrange a time for a telephone interview. The purpose of the telephone interview is to confirm responses from the pre-screening survey, to explain verbally what is involved in the study and to answer any questions you have about the study. Completion of this pre-screening survey; the telephone interview; and a subsequent baseline questionnaire, is required for all participants before they are randomized to a study group.
